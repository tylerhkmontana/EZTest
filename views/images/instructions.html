Register

When you land on our webpage, the website prompts you to register an account or login.
For first time users, either an instructor or a student, you must register an account before you can continue.

After filling out your email, password, and faculty/student id, select the appropriate radio button to
either register as a student or an instructor.



After registraion, you will be redirecrted to our homepage where you will need to login with your provided credentials to
access your account.

Instructor

After logging in, you will be redirecrted to your account homepage.
Click on the create course button to create a new course and add study material.

Fill up the form with the necessary details and click create.

After creating the new course, the course is available for you to add materials to.
You can add additional courses by clicking the create course button.
For students to access this course they will need the course code shown in the course box.

Once inside the course menu, the dashboard displays options to create announcements.
The side bar displays options to create assignments, view grades etc.

Clicking on the assignments tab opens more options. To create new assignment or to view older assignments.

To create a new assignment click on the new assignment button which opens a prompt to enter assingment name and deadline.
Before cliking the save button, click on the create question button which allows you to add questions for the
assignment.

The create question prompt allows you to create two types of questions. Selcting the multiple choice type opens another prompt to
edit question and select the correct answer.

To create more questions, click on the create question button again. (This time selecting a short answer question)

Once you have added the necessary questions and provided the answers, click on save to save the assignment which then becomes available
to students.

clicking on the assignments tab now reveals previous and current assignments.

Assignments are graded automatically. Clicking on the grades tab reveals students who have completed the assingment and their attained score.

Student

After logging in, you will be redirecrted to your account homepage.
Click on the enroll button to enroll in a course.

Cliking the enroll button opens a prompt to enter the course code. Ask your instructor for the course code for your class. Hit enroll.

Once you have entered the correct course code, the course is now available on your homepage.
To access course material, cllick enter.

On the course dashboard you can see the current assignment, its due date, and its status(whether you have completed the assignment or not).
Below the assignments you can see any announcements from your instructor.

Clicking on the announcment opens a dilaog box with the announcment from your instructor.

Assignments and gradebook is displayed on the sidebar.

Clicking on Assignment reveals more options. Under current assignment you can view the current assingment.
You can review old assingments by clicking on the previous assignments tab.

Clicking on Big-O analysis under current assignment reveals the entire entire assingment.
After completing the assignment. click submit.

YOU CAN ONLY SUBMIT AN ASSIGNMENT ONCE. Make sure to double-check your answers before you click submit.
Onc eyou have done so, click confirm!

Selecting any previous assignments from the sidebar reveals all the answers from preivious assingments.

The grades tab reveals all graded assignments.
